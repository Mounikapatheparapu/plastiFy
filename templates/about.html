About Plastify

Plastify is an AI-powered sustainability platform that identifies, sorts, and recycles plastic waste using cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning.

Who We Are

We are a team of innovators and environmental engineers dedicated to redefining waste management using intelligent automation. With AI-driven detection systems, Plastify accelerates recycling efficiency and reduces global plastic pollution.

Our aim is to build scalable recycling ecosystems that empower cities, industries, and communities to achieve a sustainable and pollution-free future.

Our Mission

We aim to make sustainable plastic handling simple, practical, and accessible for everyone.

  • Promote circular economy via intelligent reuse.
  • Reduce landfill and ocean pollution.
  • Provide transparent, data-driven insights.
  • Enable fair pricing for reusable plastics.
  • Support industrial and community recycling efforts.

With RRR Prediction, plastic becomes a resource — not refuse.

How It Works

Users input basic details about plastic waste:

  • Type of plastic (PET, HDPE, etc.)
  • Condition (cracked, stained, intact)
  • Age and weight
  • Contamination level

Our AI analyzes these inputs and outputs actionable scores for reuse, recycle, and resale — instantly guiding the best next step.

Why Choose Plastify?

  • Easy & Fast: Get insights in seconds.
  • Accurate: Built on real-world waste data.
  • Eco-Impact: Reduce your environmental footprint.
  • Economic Value: Unlock hidden resale potential.
  • Scalable: From homes to recycling plants.

RRR Prediction isn’t just technology — it’s a movement toward a cleaner planet.
